Our Vision

Knowledge is the only thing that grows when shared." Protege, is a group of people who firmly believe in this statement and are eager to build a healthy coding culture among young coders. The best thing about the internet is that there is no shortage of information; but sometimes, there is just too much information, which can be overwhelming. To streamline the amount of information in the right way can be a taxing process, but here is where we can be of assistance! Our resource/roadmaps are specially structured according to common needs and are easy to follow! All you need to bring with you is the will to learn! So what are you waiting for? Come join us!

FAQ

1What is Protege?

An organisation born from the simple idea of wanting to help people through their coding journey, Protege has come a long way. Simply put, we are here to assist you with your coding woes and turn them into joyful experiences!

2 How to join Protege as a volunteer/Mentor?

Fill the Mentor form, if your profile is found suitable and there is a matching requirement, We will contact you.

3Is it only for girls

No! Protege welcomes everyone in need of guidance irrespective of their gender

4 How to enroll in a course/codemap?

The courses are open to all and separate enrollment is not required. You can just head on to what you want to learn and start learning. All resources are here

5Who all are eligible to enroll?

Anybody who is willing to learn new skills and willing to mentor the participants are eligible to participate
